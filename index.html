About me

I am a Ph.D. research scholar in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, IIT Kharagpur working under the supervision of Prof. Bivas Mitra. I joined the department in July, 2015. My broad area of interest lies in the area of Data Science and Analysis of Online Social Networks. My research work involves the study of Information Diffusion in Online Social Networks such as Yelp and Twitter. I am a part of the Complex Network Research Group (CNeRG) in IIT Kharagpur.
